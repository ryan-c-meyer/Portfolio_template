Crop Genetic Engineering

Through the EUREKA! program with Clemson Honors, I was able to spend 10 weeks conducting research for a project titled "Crop Genetic Engineering for Enhanced Broad Abiotic Stress Tolerance in Transgenic Creeping Bentgrass" alongside Dr. Hong Luo of the Genetics and Biochemistry department at Clemson University. This research showed the benefits of the overexpression of OsSIZ1 (a rice SUMO E3 ligase gene) when creeping bentgrass was exposed to abiotic stress tolerances.
